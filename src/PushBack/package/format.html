How to Play:

1. Use your arrow keys to move your piece around.

2. There will be power-ups randomly placed on the map that you can collect to push another player further back than normal.

3. To aim is to be able to hit another player enough that they fall of the platform.

About:

Push Back is a MM0 game where the aim is for you to try to survive by knocking other players off while the map is shrinking and winning by being the last one standing on the given platform.
